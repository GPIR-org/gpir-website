Global Peace Intercede Reward

Global Peace Intercede Reward (GIPR) is a private voluntary organisation registered under PVO number 63/20. The organisation is aimed to fight against drug and substance abuse, early child marriages, gender based violence and child labour.

In doing so, GIPR assists children and youths to identify their talent, nurture and develop them so that they reach their full potential. Exposing them to equal opportunities regardless of race, background or status in society.

The organisation offers proactive approach to life skills training programmes to capacitate and empower the youths so that they can be self reliant through entrepreneurship. The organisation collaborates and works with other development partners, government and local authorities in advocacy, awareness campaigns, prevention and empowering programmes.
